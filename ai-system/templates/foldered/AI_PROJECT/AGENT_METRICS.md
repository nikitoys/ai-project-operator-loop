Status: Draft

## Purpose

This file records lightweight planning, execution, review and QA metrics for future pilot validation.

Metrics are learning inputs only.

Metrics do not authorize execution, parallel execution, automatic execution, automatic merge or automatic acceptance.

Human Owner remains the final decision maker.

## Safety Boundaries

- Sequential execution remains the default.
- Candidate parallel groups are informational until approved.
- Metrics must not trigger automatic work.
- Metrics must not override review, QA or Human Owner acceptance.
